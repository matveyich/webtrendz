branding your website

Just like any other litrature, a corporate website must be branded and fall 'inline' with your corporate message. At Webtrendz we understand this concept very well.

We are able to digest your current branding guidelines, brochures and other litrature, to provide you with a branded website that not only looks good, but one that is functional both for the user and your staff. Providing both with the help and support needed to gain the most from your website.

Ideal development stages:

  • Stage 1 - we discuss with you, your ideas and brand
  • Stage 2 - we present the 'style guide'
  • Stage 3 - we agree points and make final changes
  • Stage 4 - we present the revised 'style guide'
  • Stage 5 - we advise your design team about the 'style guide'

We are happy to create a new corporate identity for your company with our branding services - or simply give your website some time at our ‘rebranding-clinic’ to refresh and make it feel revitalised. Once you’re happy with the new ideas, we can develop the new or refreshed brand identity across a full range of promotional media, from digital to web and print.
